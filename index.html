About me

Hi, I'm Andrey – HTML coder from Kharkiv (Ukraine).
I'm interested in web development and everything connected with it.

Graduated from the Odessa National Academy of Telecommunications and received
a degree in Telecommunications Engineer.
Also graduated from technical school with a specialty Maintenance and repair of communication equipment and office equipment.
Worked as a technical support engineer for an Internet service provider. I set up switches, worked with the terminal, installed the firmware.
Also configured client computers and routers. Installed CCTV systems.

Now I'm live in Grimstad (Norway).
I started learning web development at the beginning of 2021,
at first it was my hobby, but in the last year I started learning actively.
In addition, from May 2023 to date, I work at Nymo as an intern, where I work on real-world tasks, as a full-stack C# developer.
Now I am ready for tasks related to HTML page layout (HTML/CSS-coding),
with further development and in the future I am ready to develop as a full-fledged front-end developer.
